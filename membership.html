Microsoft Gold Membership



At Newdream Data Systems, we are proud to announce that we have attained Gold partner status with Microsoft Partner Network. Microsoft Gold Partner confirms our dedication to support Microsoft platforms and technologies. This recognizes our capabilities in building and providing advanced services to web development and applications in a superior way using latest Microsoft Technologies.


Microsoft certification also ensures highly qualified and talented professionals who constantly endeavor to leverage their skills. In order to achieve Microsoft Gold status, our team took a set of tests to demonstrate the degree of their technological expertise. The partnership program also required us to submit some customer references and engage a necessary number of Microsoft Certified Professionals to adapt at current technologies significant for the competency.
